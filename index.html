About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Yukiya Nakamura and I am a student at Ritsumeikan University, ISSE (Information Systems and Science Engineering) course! Although I am not doing great in class, I still retain the burning passion for learning about computers and the engineering discipline! Even though I’m on the edge of failing many classes, I have dedicated my life to becoming a professional Valorant player, a professional bed sleeper, and a PhD in Razer lover. Jokes aside, I have experienced the Netherlands for 4 years by participating in an exchange program, engraining me with massive opportunities and perspectives of the Dutch. This event fueled the start of my endeavor to be a great English speaker, where I have been in an environment where everyone excelled at it. By joining an English course at Ritsumeikan University, I see myself seeing many peers who share similar passions, and with my clumsiness, encountering impromptu networking and opportunities to set me for studying abroad for my master's degree to gain big bucks, beautiful wife, bewilderingly lovely children, and boisterous pets! Although this is a description of me, I can’t forget my peers who have become a large part of me, and I hope to see them shine in the world of competitive brilliance. Thank you - Yukiya Nakamura 2024
